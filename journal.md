# June 24 2026

I created the schematic first, which is weird because I can't put actual inductors in the schematic since they're really traces that are coils in the PCB. So they ARE inductors but recognized as regular traces.
It's based off the Slayer Exciter circuit, which is what most tesla coil beginners use. The upside of this circuit is that it is extremely low power and self tuning.
